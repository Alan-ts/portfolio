The Class Room Assistant



About the project


The Classroom Assistant is a web-based application designed to enhance the way students and educators interact with PDF documents. It provides an intuitive platform where users can upload PDFs, ask questions about their content, and receive accurate responses using advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) models. This makes learning more interactive, efficient, and accessible for students of all levels. The Classroom Assistant bridges the gap between traditional study methods and AIpowered learning, making document-based learning more intuitive, efficient, and accessible.


Motivation


In today's digital learning environment, students and educators interact with a vast amount of reading materials, primarily in the form of PDF documents, textbooks, research papers, and lecture notes. However, traditional methods of engaging with these materials—such as manual reading, searching for information, and taking notes—can be time-consuming, inefficient, and inaccessible to many users.


The Classroom Assistant project was motivated by the need to enhance the learning experience by leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to make document-based learning more interactive, efficient, and accessible



Key Features


  • PDF Upload & Processing Users can easily upload and interact with PDF documents.
  • AI-Powered Q&A System – Provides instant answers to questions based on document content.
  • Voice Input Integration – Enables hands-free interaction for increased accessibility
  • Smart Search & Text Highlighting – Helps users quickly find key information within PDFs
  • Collaborative Learning – Supports document sharing and real-time discussions.
  • Cross-Platform Accessibility – Works on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices without requiring software installation.
